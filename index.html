Privacy Policy

AI Resume Builder - CV Maker & Career Tools

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Resume Builder ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using AI Resume Builder, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

AI Resume Builder is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

Any resume content you enter — including your name, work experience, education, and skills — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Device Permissions

The App may request access to the following device features:

  • Storage (Read/Write): Used to save generated PDF resumes and cover letters to your device. All files are created and stored locally.
  • Share Sheet: Used to share your generated PDF via messaging apps, email, or other apps of your choice. Sharing is initiated entirely by you.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We use the following third-party service that may collect certain information automatically:

  • Google AdMob: Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data for ad personalization, including device identifiers, IP address, and usage data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

3. AI-Powered Features

The App includes AI-powered features such as resume optimization, ATS scoring, cover letter generation, and interview question suggestions. These features are unlocked by watching rewarded ads and are processed to provide you with suggestions and feedback.

We do not send your resume content to any external AI server. All AI-assisted features operate using on-device logic or anonymized, non-identifiable inputs.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (resume building, CV creation, cover letter generation, ATS scoring, job tracking)
  • To save your resumes and preferences locally on your device
  • To display advertisements within the App via Google AdMob
  • To gate premium features behind rewarded ads in a user-initiated manner

5. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All your resumes, cover letters, job tracking data, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive). No data is uploaded to any external server.
  • No Cloud Upload: We do not upload, transmit, or store your personal information or documents on any external server or cloud service.
  • Ad Data: Usage and ad interaction data collected by Google AdMob is stored on Google's servers in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Google AdMob as described above, solely for advertising purposes.
  • As required by law, such as to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or respond to a lawful request from public authorities.

8. Children's Privacy

AI Resume Builder is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • The right to access the information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising (via your device settings)
  • The right to withdraw storage permissions at any time through your device settings

Since all your resume data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

10. Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Tracking, and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track".
  • Android: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads, and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App. Since your resume data never leaves your device, the primary security layer is your device's own security (passcode, Face ID, etc.). We do not transmit your documents over the Internet, which significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access.
